FAQ

Common Questions

What age groups do you train?+

We train serious athletes from U14 through professional level. Whether you're a high school sophomore preparing for college recruitment, a college-committed player looking to sharpen your game before preseason, or a professional looking to maintain elite performance — ProCarthy has a program built for your stage.

How often are training sessions?+

Schedules are customized based on your evaluation, goals, and current commitments. Most athletes train 2–4 times per week. For example, a college-bound player might do two technical sessions and one tactical session per week, while a youth ECNL player might supplement club training with one or two focused private sessions.

Is the evaluation really free?+

Yes, 100%. The evaluation session is completely free with no obligation. It's a structured session where our coaches assess your current technical ability, tactical awareness, physical conditioning, and mindset. Afterward, you'll receive honest feedback and — if it's the right fit — a personalized development plan tailored to your goals.

Which locations can I train at?+

We operate across six locations throughout Southern California: The Valley, West LA, South Bay, Palos Verdes, Long Beach, and Orange County. Athletes can train at any location based on their schedule — many of our players rotate between locations depending on the day and session type.

Do you help with college recruitment?+

Absolutely. College recruitment support is a core part of what we do. This includes building professional highlight reels, connecting athletes directly with college coaching staffs, guidance on the NCAA eligibility process, and helping players identify the right programs for their level and goals. We've placed 50+ athletes into NCAA Division I, II, and III programs — including UCLA, UC Berkeley, UPENN, Harvard, and Georgetown.

What makes ProCarthy different from other training programs?+

ProCarthy was founded by former professional players who have competed and coached at the highest levels. Every session is designed around modern demands — not generic drills. We combine technical precision, physical resilience, tactical intelligence, and mental performance into a holistic system. Our athletes have gone on to play in MLS, the Bundesliga, Liga Portugal, and top NCAA programs across the country.
