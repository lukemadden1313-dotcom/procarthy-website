FAQ

Common Questions

What age groups do you train?+

We train serious athletes of all ages committed to reaching the next level — college, semi-pro, or professional.

How often are training sessions?+

Schedules are customized based on your evaluation, goals, and commitments. Most athletes train multiple times per week.

Is the evaluation really free?+

Yes. The evaluation is completely free — designed to assess your level and determine if ProCarthy is the right fit.

Which locations can I train at?+

South Bay, Palos Verdes, Long Beach, and Orange County. Athletes can train at any location based on schedule.

Do you help with college recruitment?+

Yes. Our holistic approach includes supporting athletes through the college recruitment process — highlight reels to program connections.
