Our Story

The Names Behind ProCarthy

Co-Founder

Vincent Probosz

A former professional player who has coached at elite levels. Vincent saw the gap between average training and what high-level development truly demands.

His philosophy centers on technical precision, tactical intelligence, and the mental edge that separates good players from great ones.

Co-Founder

Dan McCarthy

With deep experience coaching at elite levels, Dan brings a holistic approach to player development — combining physical conditioning, resilience training, and game intelligence.

Together with Vincent, they built ProCarthy to be the place where serious athletes come to prepare for the highest levels of competition.
